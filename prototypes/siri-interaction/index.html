Siri Glass Orb — an interactive WebGL recreation of Apple's Siri animation

A faithful WebGL2 rebuild of the Siri experience: a draggable liquid-glass orb with refraction and edge highlights, an audio-reactive spectral waveform driven by your microphone, a six-dot thinking ring that gathers and bursts, and a glass answer pill. Click the orb, type a question, and the answer streams into the glass pill. No frameworks, no dependencies — readable GLSL and JavaScript source.
